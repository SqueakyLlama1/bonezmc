BonezMC Minecraft Server

BonezMC is a semi-vanilla survival server focused on keeping Minecraft simple, fair, and fun. No pay-to-win, no overpowered kits, and no cluttered menus. Just real survival with helpful features that respect the vanilla feel.

Lagging?

🔨 Features ⛏

BonezMC has a great selection of features that make survival easier. Such as:

• Veinminer - Mine a whole ore vein just by mining one of its ores.

• TreeFeller - Chop an entire tree just by chopping one block.

• EssentialsX - A bundle of survival commands like /home, /tpa and more!

• GriefPrevention - Claim land and more!

• ChestShop - Make shops to sell your own items at your own set price!

• PlayerWarps - Places that any player can teleport to. Such as a shop, farm, and more!

Tutorials

Claiming Land

Claiming land is an amazing way to make sure that nobody can touch your builds!
How to claim land: with a golden shovel (comes in the starter kit), right click (PC) LT (Console) or Tap (Mobile) 2 corners of where you want your land to be. Any claim must be a minimum of 3 x 3 blocks.
An example is shown below.


Trusting Players

For whatever reason you may need others to be able to build in your claim, like friends or a team building a base together, you can trust them using /trust [username]. Replace [username] with the name of the player you want to trust.
If you change your mind and decide you don't want them to access anything in your claim, you can untrust them using /untrust [username].
An example is shown below. To get a list of players that are trusted in your claim, you can use /trustlist.


Checking land Claims

In order to check if someone has claimed some land and who it is, right click (PC) LT (Console) or Tap (Mobile) the land with a stick.
An example is shown below.


Unclaiming your land

In order to get rid of land claims, you can use /unclaim or /abandonclaim
To get rid of all your land claims all at once, use /abandonallclaims and then quickly /abandonallclaims confirm
