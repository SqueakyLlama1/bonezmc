BonezMC Minecraft Server

BonezMC is a semi-vanilla survival server focused on keeping Minecraft simple, fair, and fun. No pay-to-win, no overpowered kits, and no cluttered menus. Just real survival with helpful features that respect the vanilla feel.

Join us in the fun!

Great news! BonezMC is cross-platform, meaning both Java and Bedrock Editions can play together!

â˜• Java Edition

The OG edition of Minecraft. Playable on PC.

How to join BonezMC on Java Edition:

1. Load into Minecraft (1.9 or newer)
2. Click "Multiplayer" on the main menu
3. Click "Add Server"
4. Type in the following information:
Name: BonezMC
IP Address: play.bonezmc.org
5. Click "Done"

ðŸª¨ Bedrock Edition

The universal edition of Minecraft, playable on just about anything.

How to join BonezMC on Bedrock Edition:

1. Load into Minecraft
2. Click "Play"
3. Click "Servers" on the top right of the menu
4. On the list of servers on the left, scroll all the way down and click "Add Server"
5. Enter the following information:
Name: BonezMC
IP Address: play.bonezmc.org
Port: 19132
6. Click "Save and Play"
