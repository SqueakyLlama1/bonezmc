BonezMC - Semi-Vanilla Survival

BonezMC is a semi-vanilla survival server focused on keeping Minecraft simple, fair, and fun. No pay-to-win, no overpowered kits, and no cluttered menus. Just real survival with helpful features that respect the vanilla feel.

What to expect:

Classic survival with minor quality-of-life upgrades

Land claiming to protect your builds

Crossplay support for Java and Bedrock (Port 19123)

Active backups and lag-free performance

No paid advantages, ever

Join a community that actually plays the game instead of grinding ranks or spamming crates. Build, explore, survive. The way it was meant to be.
